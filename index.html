  • October 8th, 2024

    Last weekend I decided to go walk by the bridge again. I wanted to see if I could get a closer look since the incident. I noticed it was fenced off this time. Only in late evenings and early mornings its back open again. But its odd. Nobody really goes to the spot where it happened. I asked an friend who lived up here if it was normal for people to avoid the bridge at night. I myself am from a pretty busy city so every public park/path is always semi-busy at the very least. But this, its just dead silent some hours. And I dont just mean that nobody goes there. I mean there quite literally is no sound. No birds, no wind, no cars. Nothing. I dont know what to make of it. I want to go back and check it out some more. A little busy right now with school and other stuff but I will try to make time for it.
